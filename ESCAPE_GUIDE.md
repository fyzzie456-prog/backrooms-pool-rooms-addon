# Pool Rooms - Escape & Exit Guide

## Exiting the Pool Rooms

Yes! There IS a way to escape the Pool Rooms dimension.

### 🌀 Portal Pools - Your Only Escape

There are **4 rare portal pools** hidden throughout the Pool Rooms dimension. Entering any of them will teleport you back to the Overworld.

### 📍 Portal Locations

**Portal Pool #1 - North Corridor (Difficulty: Easy)**
- Location: North hallway (~-15, 53, -130)
- Description: Purple water pool embedded in the corridor wall
- How to find: Follow the north corridor straight ahead
- Visibility: Somewhat visible if you explore thoroughly

**Portal Pool #2 - East Corridor (Difficulty: Easy-Medium)**
- Location: East hallway (~140, 53, -5)
- Description: Purple water pool on the eastern wall
- How to find: Explore the east corridor
- Visibility: Hidden but accessible if you search

**Portal Pool #3 - West Locker Area (Difficulty: Medium)**
- Location: West side, near lockers (~-135, 53, 35)
- Description: Purple water pool in the locker room area
- How to find: Navigate to the west locker room
- Visibility: Tucked away, requires thorough exploration

**Portal Pool #4 - Main Pool (Difficulty: HARD)**
- Location: Underwater in main pool (~-15, 53, -15)
- Description: Purple water pool submerged beneath the main pool
- How to find: Dive deep into the center pool and search underwater
- Visibility: Hidden underwater - hardest to find!
- Requirements: Water breathing potion or careful breath management

### 🏊 How to Escape

1. **Find a portal pool** - Search the dimension for purple water
2. **Enter the portal** - Swim or walk into the purple water
3. **Teleport occurs** - You'll be instantly teleported to the Overworld
4. **Confirmation message** - You'll see: "✓ You have escaped the Pool Rooms..."

### 💡 Tips for Finding Portals

- **Explore systematically** - Check all corridors and side areas
- **Look for purple colors** - Portal pools are distinctly purple (not cyan like regular water)
- **Go underwater** - One portal is completely submerged
- **Check dead ends** - Portals are hidden in alcoves and corners
- **Use a map** - Mark locations as you explore
- **Bring water breathing** - Useful for finding the underwater portal

### 🎮 Gameplay Strategy

**If you want to find exits easily:**
- Explore thoroughly
- Check all four cardinal directions
- Look both above and below ground level
- Use coordinates to track your position

**If you want the challenge:**
- Limit your exploration time
- No water breathing potions
- Navigate by memory
- Find the hardest (underwater) portal first

### 🔧 Technical Details

**Portal Specifications:**
- Block type: Nether portal blocks (purple animated texture)
- Location: Scattered in hidden areas
- Accessibility: All accessible without breaking blocks
- Effect: Instant teleportation to Overworld spawn (0, 64, 0)
- Message: Green success message confirming escape

### ⚠️ Important Notes

- Portal pools are **the only way to exit** the End dimension
- Once you find them, they remain in the same locations
- You can use them multiple times
- If you get stuck, creative mode provides `/kill @s` or `/tp @s 0 64 0`
- In survival, you MUST find a portal pool to escape
